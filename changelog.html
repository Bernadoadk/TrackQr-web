App updates

Changelog

Product updates for the Track QR Shopify app.

June 2026

Analytics and CSV exports

  • Connected the analytics dashboard to real scan data from the app database.
  • Added real metrics for scans over time, devices, countries, top QR codes, and recent scans.
  • Updated analytics exports so CSV files download directly instead of opening the Shopify admin app URL.
  • Made exports respect the selected reporting period, including Last N Days filters.
  • Clarified conversion chart behavior when attribution is not available on the current plan.
June 2026

QR manager improvements

  • Improved the My QR Codes page so lists use real QR code, scan, and conversion data.
  • Added filtered CSV exports for QR codes using the visible search, type, status, and sort controls.
  • Improved export reliability inside the embedded Shopify app experience.
June 2026

Campaign pages and editor

  • Added customizable campaign landing pages for QR destinations.
  • Introduced campaign page settings for layout, blocks, branding, and slug-based public pages.
  • Improved the campaign editor direction around merchant-friendly controls and better desktop layouts.
June 2026

Billing and pricing

  • Added clearer handling for Shopify billing errors so merchants see actionable messages instead of app crashes.
  • Added explicit billing test and production modes for review, staging, and live subscriptions.
  • Added a visible billing mode indicator on the Pricing page.
Initial app release

Core QR tracking foundation

  • Created the core Track QR Shopify app experience.
  • Added dynamic QR code creation, scan tracking, redirects, and campaign organization.
  • Added the first analytics, QR management, pricing, and public redirect routes.
  • Added Shopify webhook handling for app lifecycle, orders, customers, and privacy events.
