Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Overview

Track QR helps merchants create dynamic QR codes and understand campaign performance. This policy explains the information we may collect, how it is used, and the choices available to merchants and visitors.

Information We Collect

We may process account information, store details, campaign settings, QR destination URLs, scan events, device signals, timestamps, approximate location information, and analytics needed to operate the service.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide QR code generation, dynamic redirects, campaign analytics, support, security monitoring, product improvement, and compliance with legal obligations.

Shopify Data

When Track QR is used with Shopify, we only access the data required to install, authenticate, and provide the app experience. Store data is used to operate Track QR features and is not sold.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain business records, resolve disputes, and meet legal requirements. Merchants may request deletion of eligible data.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to bernadoecom@gmail.com.
